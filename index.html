Founder Personal Branding
Most founders fail at hiring not because of bad talent.
But because of a broken onboarding framework.
I spent 4 years making this exact mistake. Hiring top talent, giving them a laptop, and expecting magic. Result? High churn, low morale, and wasted capital. Here is the 3-step "Ownership Framework" I switched to: 1. The Day 1 Victory: Give them a micro-task they can complete and win within their first 4 hours. Confidence is immediate. 2. Async Context Vault: Build a Notion repo for everything. Never let a new hire wait for a meeting to get an answer. 3. The 30-Day Guardrail: Define what "Failure" looks like, not just success. Clear boundaries eliminate anxiety. If you don't scale your onboarding, you can never scale your business. Agree? Or do you prefer the 'sink or swim' method? Let me know below.
